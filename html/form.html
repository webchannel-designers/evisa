14

Single
Entry

14 Days Visa:This is recommended for visitors planning a short stay In Dubai or UAE for Meetings, Conferences or stopovers whilst in transit. Requirements for obtaining the 14 Days Visa varies depending on nationality. Employment is strictly forbidden on this visa.

Type of Visa 14 Days Visa Single Entry
Visa Validity 60 days from the date of issue
Stay Validity 14 days from the date of entry
Processing Time 24 - 72 Working Hours

Required Documents

  • Colour scanned copy of the visa applicant(s) passport with at least 6 months validity
  • A clear colour photograph of visa applicant(s)
  • Colour copy of the Guarantor’s passport
  • Colour copy of the Guarantor’s valid UAE residency visa
  • Front and back copy of Guarantor’s Emirates ID
  • Completed Guarantee form

Note : We will advise you should any additional documents be required by the UAE Immigration authorities.
